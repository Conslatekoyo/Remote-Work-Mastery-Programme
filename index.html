What is ARWMP

Africa's Premier Remote Work Intensive

The African Remote Work Mastery Program (ARWMP) is a 4-week, fully virtual intensive program designed to equip African professionals with the skills, tools, mindset, and networks to compete and thrive in the global remote work economy.

From remote job hunting to freelancing on global platforms to launching remote-first African ventures, ARWMP gives you the exact roadmap — Africa-specific strategy built for real challenges.

Delivered by 54Links — the pan-African professional networking platform Powering Africa's Business Connections.

Who It's For

Early-career to mid-level African professionals across all 54 AU member states who are ready to:

  • Break into global remote employment
  • Build a serious freelance income
  • Launch a remote-first entrepreneurial venture
  • Transition from traditional to remote work
  • Upskill with AI tools and global work standards

Program Goals

  • Equip participants with a world-class remote professional mindset
  • Master tools used by top global remote teams

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining ARWMP.

What is the African Remote Work Mastery Program?
ARWMP is a 4-week, fully virtual intensive program designed and delivered by 54Links to equip African professionals with the skills, tools, and strategies to thrive in the global remote work economy. It covers remote mindset, AI tools, job hunting, freelancing, and entrepreneurship.
Who is this program designed for?
ARWMP is designed for early-career to mid-level African professionals across all 54 AU member states. Whether you're looking for remote employment, want to build a freelance business, or launch a remote-first venture, there is a track tailored for you.
What are the three career tracks?
Track A (Remote Employment) is for professionals seeking global remote jobs. Track B (Freelancing) is for those building a freelance business on Upwork, Fiverr, etc. Track C (Remote Entrepreneurship) is for those launching a remote-first African business. All tracks share Modules 1–3 and 5–9; only Module 4 diverges.
How is the program delivered?
The program is 60% live via Zoom and 40% asynchronous via Google Classroom. Live sessions run Monday to Friday. Async tasks are completed in the afternoons. Mentor office hours are every Wednesday 4–5 PM EAT. Guest speakers appear every Friday.
What time zone are the live sessions in?
Live sessions are scheduled in East Africa Time (EAT). Please confirm your local time zone equivalent before enrolling to ensure compatibility with your schedule.
How much does the program cost?
The program fee is $1,100 USD — a one-time investment that includes all 9 modules, the Capstone Project, Demo Day participation, and your ARWMP Certificate of Completion issued by 54Links.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept payments via Paystack (supporting Visa/Mastercard, M-Pesa, MTN Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, USSD and more) and PayPal. You can also email 54Linksremoteworkmastery@gmail.com for alternative payment arrangements.
Will I receive a certificate?
Yes. Upon successful completion of the full 4-week program — including the Capstone Project and Demo Day — you will receive an official ARWMP Certificate of Completion issued by 54Links.
What happens after I pay and enrol?
After payment is confirmed, you'll receive a welcome email with your cohort details, Slack workspace invitation, Google Classroom access, and your programme schedule. The program begins June 7, 2026.
What tools will I need?
You'll need a laptop or computer with a reliable internet connection, Zoom, a Google account, and Slack. All other tools (Notion, Trello, ChatGPT, Claude, Loom, etc.) are introduced during the program curriculum.
What is the accountability group?
Every participant is placed in an accountability group of 8–12 peers with a dedicated Slack channel, Tuesday and Friday peer check-in sessions, and mutual support throughout the 4 weeks.
Is there a refund policy?
The program fee is non-refundable once your spot is confirmed. Please review the program details carefully before enrolling. For any concerns, contact 54Linksremoteworkmastery@gmail.com.
